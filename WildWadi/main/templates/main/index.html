Wild Wadi Waterpark™

Wild Wadi Waterpark™ welcomes you and your family back for unforgettable aquatic adventures in one of Dubai’s most iconic waterparks





Visit one of Dubai's most cozy and favorite water parks - Wild Wadi. Located next to the world-famous Burj al-Arab Hotel, it will give you a sea of great mood, an adrenaline rush and wonderful attractions for visitors of all ages. The name of the park has long been a household name, and the complex itself is considered almost the most popular water park in the UAE. In addition to everything will be a magnificent view of the surrounding attractions and a huge swimming pool with an artificial wave imitating the sea wave.
